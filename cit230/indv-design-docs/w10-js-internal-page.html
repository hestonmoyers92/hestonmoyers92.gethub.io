The Reason Behind my company.

My name is Heston Moyers and I have been married to my beautiful wife Ashley for almost 4 years. We have an amazing 2 year old that has been the best thing that has happened to us. Here is a picture of my little family.

When me and my wife got married we decided to go to ikea and get some new furniture with the money we received from our wedding. This was a huge mistake. the furniture did not last long at all and we were faced with a situation where we could not afford to buy new furniture. as time went by when livng with old broken furniture i decided to take action into my own hands and instead of paying a fortune for furniture that wil actally work i decided to look on craigslist for old furniture that was strong and sturdy and refinished it. here is a picture of the verry first piece of furniture that i refinished.

Once I refinished that piece of furniture I started planning to build some furniture of my own. So gradually I started collecting the tools that i needed to make the furniture and as time went on I pretty much had all the furniture in my house hand made for a fraction of the cost.
